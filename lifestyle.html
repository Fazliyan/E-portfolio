Pasta with Avocado Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 2 servings of spaghetti
  • Optional: cherry tomatoes, basil, cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta and drain.
  2. Blend avocado, garlic, lemon, oil, salt & pepper.
  3. Toss with pasta and garnish. Serve warm.
